Build instructions

Mac OSX

Configure the Ming lib (command line):

./configure --prefix=/Users/Simon/extern/ --disable-freetype CFLAGS="-isysroot /Developer/SDKs/MacOSX10.5.sdk -arch i386 -mmacosx-version-min=10.5" LDFLAGS="-syslibroot,/Developer/SDKs/MacOSX10.5.sdk"

This is done in order to build for OSX 10.5 from OSX 10.6
